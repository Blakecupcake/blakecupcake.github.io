Creative Designer and all things Fashion lover

Courtney Blake, who creates under the pseudonym Blakecupcake, is a fashion guru in the making. Her rich history in fashion, marketing, and design has led her to work with several growing fashion brands in Metro Detroit and even design a popular storefront into a women's walk-in closet and fashion boutique. Her work has prompted many innovative and unique marketing and branding campaigns, and her passion to support small brands is showcased in her many collaborative projects.

Whether your looking for new trendy fashion, vintaged treasures or up-cycled recreations, you've come to the right place!
